Block Surfer - Minecraft Style Endless Runner

Welcome to Block Surfer, the ultimate 3D endless runner game inspired by the world of Minecraft. Run through procedurally generated biomes including lush forests, scorching deserts, snowy tundras, and the fiery nether.

Gameplay Features:

  • Endless 3D running action with Minecraft-style blocky graphics.
  • Collect coins to increase your score and unlock power-ups.
  • Power-ups: Jetpack for flying, Magnet for coin attraction, Sneakers for high jumps, and 2X Multiplier.
  • Dynamic biomes that change as you progress.
  • High score tracking to compete with yourself and others.
  • Optimized for both desktop (keyboard) and mobile (swipe gestures).
